## Overview
3-page interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 2,000 orders across 3 global regions 
(Asia Pacific, Europe, North America) for NovaMart — a fictional D2C e-commerce company.
Built to surface sales performance, regional gaps, return rate issues, and growth opportunities.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI Desktop** — Dashboard design and publishing
- **DAX** — Custom measures (Profit Margin %, Return Rate %, CALCULATE, DIVIDE)
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning and type validation
- **Star Schema** — Fact + 3 dimension tables

---

## Dashboard Pages

**Page 1 — Executive Summary**
KPI cards, regional sales bar chart, monthly trend line, segment donut chart, top 10 products table

**Page 2 — Regional & Returns Analysis**
Return rate KPIs, returns by region, shipping cost by region, least selling products table

**Page 3 — Insights & Actions**
5 consultant-level findings with direct business actions

| Total Orders | 2,000 |
| Top Region | Asia Pacific ($2.5M) |

---

## Key Findings
1. **Asia Pacific leads revenue** at $2.5M but carries the highest return rate at 10%
2. **Consumer segment drives 39.83%** of total revenue — priority retention target
3. **June shows consistent sales dip** — mid-year promotional opportunity identified
4. **Asia Pacific shipping cost** ($24K) directly compressing margins — logistics review needed
5. **Nova Accessorie 14** is weakest product in Europe at $6,410 — discontinuation candidate

## Business Recommendations
1. Run targeted Corporate promotions in North America to close gap with Asia Pacific
2. Prioritize Consumer-focused campaigns to protect majority revenue stream
3. Introduce mid-year promotional offers to counter June sales dip
4. Investigate top returned products — implement quality checks before dispatch
5. Discontinue or replace Nova Accessorie 14 with higher demand products
